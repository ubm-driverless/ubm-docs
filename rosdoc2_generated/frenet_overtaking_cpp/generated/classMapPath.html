std::tuple<float, float, float, float> search_nearest_point(State &state)

Searches for the nearest point to the current position specified in the state.

Parameters:

state – State object representing the current position of the vehicle with middle_x and middle_y.

Returns:

std::tuple<float, float, float, float> A tuple containing:

  • current_x (float): The x-coordinate of the nearest point on the path.

  • current_y (float): The y-coordinate of the nearest point on the path.

  • s[nearest_point] (float): The path position of the nearest point.

  • distance_n (float): The normal distance between the vehicle and the path.
